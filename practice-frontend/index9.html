List and Table Tags in HTML

Types of Lists

1. Unordered List (ul)

  • First item
  • Second item
    • Nested item 1
    • Nested item 2
  • Third item

2. Ordered List (ol)

  1. First step
  2. Second step
    1. Sub-step a
    2. Sub-step b
  3. Third step

Different Ordered List Types:

  1. Numbers (default)
  1. Uppercase Letters
  1. Lowercase Letters
  1. Uppercase Roman
  1. Lowercase Roman

3. Description List (dl)
